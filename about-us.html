ABOUT US

The Heart and Soul Foundation is a registered Non-Profit Company established in 2021 to adress the needs of disadvantaged, underserved, and underprivileged children, and individuals of all ages from Quellerie Park, Burgershoop, Apple Park and Krugersdorp West in Gauteng.

Our mission is to create sustainable solutions that uplift and empower these communities through various initiatives such as education, healthcare, social welfare, and community development programs. The Heart and Soul Foundation recognizes the importance of addressing the root causes of poverty and inequality in these areas, and aims to work collaboratively with other organizations and stakeholders to bring about positive change. Through their efforts, they hope to improve the quality of life and opportunities for those in need, and to build a brighter future for the communities they serve.

By providing support in key areas, the Heart and Soul Foundation aims to positively impact the lives of those in disadvantaged communities. With a collaborative approach, they strive to create sustainable solutions that empower individuals of all ages.
