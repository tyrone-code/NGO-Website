Mission Statment

The Heart and Soul Foundation solely exists to serve the community, especially the disadvantaged children, with the help of our donors and sponsors. To do this in a sustainable manner, we believe in excellent communication, leadership and human relations.

Through our different programs we will ensure the growth of individuals, participation, and pride in the community.

At the Heart and Soul Foundation, we recognize the significance of providing meals for both our preschool and after-school children, as well as the wider community. Our commitment is to ensure that basic needs are met daily. We are also committed to supporting individuals in their job search, empowering them towards upwards mobility and growth.

Our focus will also be on the elderly population in our community, specifically those residing in old age homes. We understand that older individuals face unique challenges and have distinct needs. As such, we will conduct regular evaluations of our programs for the elderly to ensure their specific needs are met effectively.

Whenever necessary, professionals from the community will provide counseling services to individuals who require support for issues related to substance abuse, gender-based violence, and family matters

Together we will make a difference.
